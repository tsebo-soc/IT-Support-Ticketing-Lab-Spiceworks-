# IT-Support-Ticketing-Lab-Spiceworks-
Lab Activities:

Ticket Creation✅:

Created support tickets by recording user issues, assigning priorities, and documenting relevant information.

Incident Response:

Investigated reported issues, updated ticket status, and documented troubleshooting performed.

Ticket Escalation:

Escalated complex incidents to higher support tiers when first-line resolution was not appropriate.

Ticket Resolution:

Resolved issues, confirmed successful outcomes, documented solutions, and closed tickets.

What I Learned

* Importance of accurate ticket documentation.
* Following the complete incident lifecycle.
* Prioritizing and categorizing support requests.
* When and how to escalate incidents.
* Maintaining clear communication throughout the support process.
